My top 3 movies of all time

Lord of the Rings

This is quite possibly the movie that had the most impact on me while growing up.
My love for any kind of fantasy or it's sub-genres can be traced back to very movie.

Dune

A rather recent movie but the visuals, story and music has left a lasting impact on me.
One of the best sci-fi movies of recent time, that once again spark anew my love for the genre.

La La Land

The movie that probably hurt me the most of all the romance movies I have seen.
It was just so good in direction, the way their love evolved and the unfortuante turn of events that sepereated them.
